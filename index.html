What We Do at e-Yantra

We focus on national-level competitions to drive innovation and practical skills.

e-Yantra Robotics Competition (eYRC)

Participate in the prestigious annual robotics competition hosted by IIT Bombay, solving real-world problems through robotics.

e-Yantra Innovation Challenge (eYIC)

Transform your eYRC project into a viable startup. We guide teams in prototyping and pitching their innovative solutions.

SVPC-eYantra Competition

An intra-college event to nurture budding talent, providing a platform for students to showcase their skills and creativity.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in cultivating the next generation of innovators who will shape the future through robotics and AI. We aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical application, contributing to India's technological advancement.
