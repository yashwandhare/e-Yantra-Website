What We Do at e-Yantra

Explore our areas of expertise and the technologies that drive innovation

Robotics Development

Design and build autonomous robots for competitions and real-world applications using cutting-edge sensors and actuators.

Embedded Systems

Program microcontrollers and single-board computers like Arduino and Raspberry Pi for IoT and automation projects.

Computer Vision

Implement machine learning algorithms and OpenCV for object detection, tracking, and autonomous navigation systems.

Competition Training

Prepare teams for national robotics competitions including e-Yantra Robotics Competition by IIT Bombay.

IoT Solutions

Create smart campus solutions using sensor networks, cloud computing, and real-time data monitoring systems.

Collaborative Learning

Foster teamwork through hands-on workshops, peer learning, and collaborative project development.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in cultivating the next generation of innovators and engineers who will shape the future through robotics, artificial intelligence, and cutting-edge technology. We envision a world where our club members become pioneers in solving real-world challenges, bridging the gap between academic knowledge and practical application, and contributing to India's technological advancement on a global scale.
